Golden Gate Bridge

$0

A visit to the iconic Golden Gate Bridge in San Francisco. The bridge is very large and painted in a surprisingly vivid shade of red. It spans the bay which gives its name to the so-called "bay area"

$70

A beautiful museum in Golden Gate park. It features a biodome in which you climb through levels of the rainforest before descending beneath the aquarium to watch the scene from below.
